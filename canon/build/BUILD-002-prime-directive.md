## The question

Every decision in the novel must answer:

> **Does this increase discovery?**

---

## Discovery vs mystery

| Mystery | Discovery |
|---|---|
| Ends when solved | Continues after revelation |
| Closes the door | Opens the next question |
| Puzzle completion | Perception transformation |

---

## Relationship to other filters

- **BUILD-000.002 One Question:** *Does this increase wonder without reducing clarity?*
- **BUILD-002 Prime Directive:** *Does this increase discovery?*

Both must pass. Wonder without clarity fails. Discovery without wonder fails.
