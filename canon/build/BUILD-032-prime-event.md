## 032.0 — Purpose

The Prime Event is the **single historical moment** from which the entire novella grows.

Not necessarily the largest — the **most consequential**. Every major mystery · symbol · journey · misunderstanding · civilization · relationship · reread ultimately traces **back here**.

The Prime Event becomes the **hidden center of gravity**. *(CF-CON-665 · CF-CON-680 · CF-CON-649 Foundational event populated · CF-CON-677 First story-specific BUILD)*

*(Aligns: BUILD-031 Secret History archive · BUILD-015 Resonance Matrix convergence · BUILD-026 gravitational center)*

---

## 032.1 — The First Law

The Prime Event must be **ordinary enough to be overlooked**.

Obviously important → readers **wait** for it. Almost incidental → readers later realize it **quietly governed everything**.

**Recognition replaces surprise.** *(CF-CON-666 · CF-CON-681 · BUILD-018 Recognition not puzzle · CF-CON-415)*

---

## 032.2 — The Invisible Center

The Prime Event should **rarely be discussed directly**.

Readers encounter **consequences** — not explanations. Only near the end do they realize they have always been **orbiting the same unseen center**. *(CF-CON-667 · CF-CON-680 · BUILD-026 Invisible History)*

## Possibility — never shown directly *(CF-CON-678 · CF-CON-679)*

> *Not a rule — a possibility under consideration.*

What if the Prime Event is **never shown directly**?

Not in flashback · dream · exposition. The reader **reconstructs** it entirely from echoes · memories · symbols · consequences · changing perspectives.

If accomplished **fairly** *(CF-CON-656)* — the reader doesn't learn the truth. They **assemble** it.

The central revelation becomes something they **genuinely create within their own mind** — consistent with BUILD-001 through BUILD-005 from the first build.
