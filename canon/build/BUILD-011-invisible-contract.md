## What the reader unconsciously believes

- The book begins on page one.
- The book ends on the last page.
- The story is chronological.
- Characters are who I think they are.
- Reality behaves normally.

---

## Our method

We **never state** these assumptions.

We simply **allow** them.

That's our contract.

## Relationship to No Beginning

The continuous opening/closing dyad and No Beginning law subvert this contract **fairly** — never by lying.
