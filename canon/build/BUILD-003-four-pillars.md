# BUILD-003 — The Four Pillars

> **Version 1.0.0 · Status: FROZEN**
> CF-CON-159 · CF-ENTRY-036

Everything we build must strengthen **one or more** pillars.

---

## Pillar One — Wonder

Readers should continually feel that reality is **larger** than they imagined.

**Never confusion. Wonder.**

---

## Pillar Two — Humanity

The story continually strips away labels until only human beings remain.

Readers realize they supplied many labels themselves.

*(Aligns: Article XVI · Article XVII · BUILD-007)*

---

## Pillar Three — Resonance

Nothing exists only once. Every important thing **echoes**:

Words · Objects · Places · Dreams · Silence · Emotion

Readers feel coherence long before they consciously detect it.

*(Aligns: CF-CON-094 Resonance · Echo Ledger · Phase 14 Roadmap)*

---

## Pillar Four — Transformation

The book succeeds only if readers finish with a **different way of seeing**.

Not different opinions. **Different perception.**

*(Aligns: BUILD-000.001 North Star · System Seven · Reader Arc Stage Five)*
