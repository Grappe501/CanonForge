## 018.0 — Purpose

Every reader experiences the story in fragments. Every consciousness experiences life in fragments.

The Fracture Engine defines how those fragments are **arranged**, **revealed**, and eventually **integrated**.

The fracture is not damage. It is **perspective**.

## 018.1 — The First Principle

**Reality remains complete. The observer does not.**

Every consciousness sees only part of the whole. The story never repairs reality — it **expands perception**.

## 018.2 — The Five Fractures

Every important fracture belongs to one of **five domains**. *(CF-CON-404)*

| Domain | Governs |
|---|---|
| **Chronological** | Events encountered out of sequence — reading order ≠ occurrence order |
| **Memory** | Remembered event incomplete until later context reshapes meaning |
| **Identity** | Person understood partially at first — understanding revises over time |
| **Relationship** | Two consciousnesses see the same connection differently — neither has the whole |
| **Meaning** | Event appears to mean one thing · later acquires depth **without the event changing** |

*(Aligns: BUILD-009 Five Times · BUILD-016 collision · BUILD-008 assumption · BUILD-014 meaning)*

## 018.3 — The Fracture Curve

Every fracture follows the same pattern. *(CF-CON-405)*

**Integration** replaces resolution. Understanding **expands** — it does not simply end.
