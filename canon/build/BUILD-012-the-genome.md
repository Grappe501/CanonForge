## 012.18 — The Real Subject *(CF-ENTRY-048 epilogue)*

Story, characters, rhythm, silence, typography, assumptions, time, reading, humanity — these are not the subject of Chronoforge. They are expressions of something older:

> **How do human beings create meaning?**

In memories · relationships · history · identity · hope — and through reading itself:

> **How does a human mind take incomplete experience and weave it into a meaningful life?**

If we remain faithful to that question, Chronoforge stops being *"a science-fiction novella with hidden layers."* It becomes an exploration of what it means to be human through the very act of reading.
