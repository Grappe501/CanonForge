## Resonance (CF-CON-094)

**Central metaphor of the entire project.**

Not time · not memory · not space — **Resonance.**

Every event · word · silence · typographic anomaly · emotional beat vibrates across the book like waves through an instrument.

```txt
NOT:  reader collects clues → assembles truth
IS:   reader hears harmony that was present all along
```

New melodies on each play (each read).
